INTRODUCTION

Based in Vancouver, B.C., Ron Handford brings a lifelong passion for the creation of images of people, events, and natural and built environments to photography and videography projects.

Combined with a background in entrepreneurship, international business, and investor relations, Ron, through Handford Imaging, assists individuals and corporate clients in communicating their message and works to the world visually through creative and powerful imaging. As well, he portrays individuals, families, and their life events through insightful and beautiful imaging, connecting the viewer to the essence of the people and their stories.
