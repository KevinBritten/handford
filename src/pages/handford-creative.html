INTRODUCTION

Handford Creative is built on the foundation of 10 years experience in Vancouver’s built environment industry.

  Through Handford Creative, Principal Graham Handford provides architects, builders, and realtors strong graphic communication for both their companies and their projects through professional creative, imaging, and marketing services.

    STEP ONE

    Competitive Analysis

    Finalizing the generalized flow of your site including page's names and the order of information you'd like to present.

    STEP THREE

    Exploring multiple concept directions, all sharing a common goal of elevating your brand's presence online.
